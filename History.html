YouTube Subscription History

With this website you can find out when you subscribed which YouTube channel.

1 Your channel

Please enter a valid YouTube channel URL (e.g. https://www.youtube.com/user/YouTube)
Where do I find this URL?
In the YouTube App
Go to your channel by pressing your profile picture and selecting "My Channel". Then find the "Share" option in the menu and choose "Copy link".
On the website
Go to your channel overview. You get there by clicking/pressing your profile picture. Then copy the link from the address bar. It looks like "youtube.com/channel/U...".

2 Make your subscriptions public

Go to your Privacy Settings, uncheck "Keep all my subscriptions private" and click the "Save" button. You can undo it again as soon as you've finished using this website.

Subscriptions

Here you can check how long you've been subscribed to a YouTube channel. YouTube.com doesn't offer a way to show the time you clicked the subscribe button on their website, but for developers, they have an API which returns a list of all subscriptions - with the date you subscribed it!
I made this website because I wanted to know for how long I'm subscribed to my channels and what my first subscription was, but I couldn't find a way to do it. Then I found the way in the API and created this page so everyone can easily use it.
